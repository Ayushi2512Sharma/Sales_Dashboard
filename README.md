# Sales_Dashboard

Complete overview of sales performance including KPIs, trends, and geographic distribution.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) :
Displays important metrics such as Total Revenue, Quantity Sold, Customer Count, and Unit Price.

Revenue Analysis by Month :
Shows monthly revenue trends, helping identify growth patterns and seasonal variations.

Sales Distribution by Country :
Highlights top-performing countries contributing to overall sales.

Customer Distribution (Geographical View) :
Visualizes customer locations across the world, providing geographic insights.

Unit Price Trends :
Tracks changes in unit price over time to understand pricing trends.
